# The Echo Chamber (`echo-chamber`)

Debate & Consensus where all agents quickly agree. If you want real debate, assign genuinely different perspectives and reward disagreement in early rounds. **Corrective:** **Adversarial Review** (structurally forces opposition rather than hoping for organic disagreement).
